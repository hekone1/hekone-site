HEKONE Origin

Smart Harvest & Source Capture

HEKONE Origin enables real-time measurement at the point where materials enter the system. Instead of relying on delayed or centralized measurement, data is captured directly at the source — during harvesting or initial handling.

By associating weight with location and time, operations gain visibility into where materials originate, how they move, and where loss or inefficiencies occur.

Key Capabilities

Real-Time Measurement

Capture weight instantly at the point of collection.

Source Traceability

Link materials to specific locations, rows, or origins.

Loss Visibility

Identify discrepancies between expected and actual yield.

Operational Insight

Build data around performance across locations and time.

Part of the HEKONE Platform

HEKONE Origin extends the same core system used in HEKONE Smart Dispenser. Together, they enable visibility from input to output — from source to consumption.
