Product Overview

Start with the bin. Build visibility across the orchard.

HEKONE Origin begins with a smart harvest bin that measures filled weight in the field and sends it to a live system without waiting for a later weighing step.

A measurement point at the source

Each harvest bin becomes a live source of operational data. As product is collected, the system can record weight, time, and harvest context immediately.

This makes source data available the moment work happens — creating a stronger base for traceability, comparison, and decision-making later in the process.

Real-time weight measurement in the field
Wireless transmission to the live dashboard
Designed to connect harvest activity with location context
A harvest bin becomes a live data point, not just a container.
Live Operations

See harvest activity across the orchard — as it happens.

Instead of waiting for delayed reports, teams can monitor where bins are located, how much each bin contains, and which areas are outperforming or falling behind.

Operational visibility that supports real decisions

HEKONE Origin can turn bin activity into a map-based operational view. Growers and managers can see the location of bins in the orchard and track live harvest weight by zone, block, or team.

This creates a clearer picture of actual field performance, making it easier to spot uneven activity and respond faster during the workday.

Performance Insight

Compare bins in real time and identify where output is stronger or weaker.

Not every harvest zone performs the same way. HEKONE Origin can help visualize which bins are filling faster, which are lagging, and where attention may be needed.

From weights to performance signals

By comparing live weight across multiple bins, teams can quickly see which harvest points are above average, slightly below, or clearly underperforming.

This is where simple measurement becomes operational intelligence — giving managers a faster read on productivity, consistency, and field-level variation.

Identify top-performing and low-performing harvest bins
Track average versus actual output in the field
Support better staffing, routing, and inspection decisions
Performance visibility helps surface stronger and weaker harvest activity in real time.
Trace Back to Source

When a quality problem appears, trace it back to where it started.

Downstream quality issues are far more valuable when they can be linked back to the harvest area that produced them. HEKONE Origin helps connect the problem bin to its source location on the map.

From damaged fruit to a defined problem area

If a bin shows a higher rate of damaged, rotten, or pest-affected fruit, the system can highlight where that bin came from and reveal the block or rows associated with the issue.

This helps teams move from reacting to symptoms to targeting the source — improving inspection, intervention, and future prevention.

Flag bins with abnormal damage rates
Trace affected bins back to harvest zones
Focus field inspection where risk appears concentrated
A quality issue becomes an actionable field signal, not just a downstream surprise.
