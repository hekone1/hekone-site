Blind spot

The shelf is often where visibility breaks down.

In many physical environments, product leaves the shelf or dispensing point without reliable real-time measurement. Systems may report inventory later, but they often miss what actually happened in the moment.

What goes wrong

  • Consumption happens without direct measurement.
  • Inventory updates can lag behind real events.
  • Reported counts may not match physical reality.
  • Leakage, waste, or shrink can stay invisible.

Why it matters

  • Operators lose confidence in what the system reports.
  • Replenishment decisions become reactive.
  • Exception handling happens too late.
  • Accountability is weakened at the exact handoff point that matters.
Instrumentation logic

Capturing physical activity as a usable signal.

The role of this prototype is simple: detect product movement at the point of interaction and convert that change into structured signal data that can feed the larger HEKONE layer.

Measure

Weight-based detection monitors change directly at the dispensing point instead of relying on delayed manual input.

Structure

Each physical event can be translated into usable operational data that supports exception tracking, visibility, and reporting.

Connect

The captured signal can feed a broader HEKONE workflow, where physical events become part of a measurable operational system.

System role

Part of the HEKONE system — not the whole system.

Smart Gravity Bin is best understood as a signal-generating node. It does not define the platform. It contributes one stream of visibility for one type of operational blind spot.

Platform view

Beyond a single device.

HEKONE is not built around one device, one sensor, or one workflow. Different use cases may require different forms of instrumentation — from weight-based detection to movement tracking, entry monitoring, or other signal capture methods.

Targeted by design

Instrument only where visibility matters most, instead of forcing heavy infrastructure everywhere.

Built for extension

The same system logic can expand from one monitored point into multiple handoff points across the operation.

Platform-first

The long-term value is not the device alone. The value is the operational intelligence layer built on top of physical signals.

Scalability

From one measurable point to broader operational control.

The same principle can extend across factory, distribution, store, and shelf environments — creating a unified layer of measurable, AI-enabled operational intelligence across fragmented physical workflows.
